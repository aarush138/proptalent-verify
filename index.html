🔐 Aadhaar Verification

Verify your identity using Aadhaar Offline eKYC — a government-issued digitally signed document. Your Aadhaar number is never shared with us.
Step 1: Open myaadhaar.uidai.gov.in/offline-ekyc on your phone
Step 2: Login with your Aadhaar number + OTP sent to registered mobile
Step 3: Create any 4-digit Share Code and download the ZIP file
Step 4: Upload the ZIP file and enter your Share Code below
